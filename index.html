Dataset¶

Taiwan Air Quality Index Data 2016~2024

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a standardized indicator used to measure air quality in relation to various pollutants. The AQI levels range from 0 to 500, with higher values indicating worse air quality and potential health risks. The pollutants tracked in this dataset include: PM2.5, PM10, SO2, NOx, CO and O3.

Intoduction to My Projecct¶

We're curious about which county has the highest average AQI and which one has recorded the highest AQI ever. To find out, we'll create two charts: one showing the average AQI and another showing the highest AQI across the counties.

Average AQI by County¶

We found that Kaohsiung City has the highest average AQI. Among the counties with average AQI higher than 60, all except Kinmen and Lienchiang are located in southern Taiwan, which suggests that people living in southern Taiwan may face higher health risks.

Pseudocode¶

  1. Calculate the average AQI for each county, sort the results, and store them in a new variable called county_mean.
  2. Use county_mean to create a bar chart with the average AQI on the x-axis and the counties on the y-axis.

Highest AQI by County¶

We found that both Changhua and Yunlin counties have recorded the highest AQI ever (500.0), which suggests that people living in these areas should pay attention to air quality on certain days.

Pseudocode¶

  1. Find the highest AQI for each county, sort the results, and store them in a new variable called county_max.
  2. Use county_max to create a bar chart with the highest AQI on the x-axis and the counties on the y-axis.
In [7]:
county_max = subset.loc[subset.groupby('county')['aqi'].idxmax()].copy()

county_max = county_max.sort_values(by='aqi', ascending=False)

plt.figure(figsize=(10, 6))
sns.barplot(x='aqi', y='county', data=county_max)

plt.title('Highest AQI Ever Recorded by County')
plt.xlabel('Maximum AQI')
plt.ylabel('County')
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()
